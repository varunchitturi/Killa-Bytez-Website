ABOUT US

FTC TEAM #14663



The “Killabytez” is an FTC team, based in Fremont, CA, composed of dedicated students who want to explore the world of STEM. Our team started its journey in the summer of 2018, when we were all freshman in highschool. We were completely new to robotics in many aspects such as engineering, building, and coding. Throughout our first two seasons we ran into many challenges, hitting dead ends in almost every aspect of the robot, but we persevered and were able to design one of our most complex, functional robots so far. However, our team was met with bad luck as we were not able to compete in our 3rd competition and were not able to use the robot to its fullest. This new season though will be much different since we are ready and motivated to craft one of our best robots and seasons yet. We will be combining the knowledge that we have gained from our first two seasons and aim for our goal: to get the the FTC Worlds Competition!
